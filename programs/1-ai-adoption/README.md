# AI Adoption

> *Closing the gap between AI experimentation and enterprise-scale value creation.*

## Overview

88% of organizations are using AI in at least one function. Yet only 5–6% create real, measurable value at scale. The difference is not the technology — it is the organizational readiness, mindset, and execution discipline to absorb AI into how work actually gets done.

This program provides the research foundations, diagnostic frameworks, and implementation roadmaps for leaders and change agents driving meaningful AI adoption inside their organizations.

---

## 🎯 Target Audience

- **C-level & Executive Leaders**: CEOs, CTOs, IT Directors building AI strategy
- **Business Unit Leads**: Responsible for AI adoption within a function or department
- **AI Program Managers**: Overseeing enterprise-wide AI initiatives
- **Technology Consultants**: Advising clients on AI transformation

---

## 📚 Key Learning Outcomes

1. Understand **why most AI projects fail** — and the patterns that distinguish successful organizations
2. Apply the **AI-First Mindset**: redesigning work *with* AI as the default, not an add-on
3. Conduct a **SOW Audit** to measure current AI augmentation potential in any team
4. Use the **5-Level AI Maturity Framework** to diagnose where your organization stands
5. Build an **18-month implementation roadmap** from Foundation to Transformation
