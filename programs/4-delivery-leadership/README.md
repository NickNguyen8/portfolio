# Delivery Leadership

> *The skills that turn good technologists into great delivery leaders.*

## Overview

Technical expertise gets you into the room. Delivery leadership keeps you there. The ability to manage ambiguity, align stakeholders, build delivery confidence, and lead teams through complexity is not taught in computer science programs — it is learned through experience, often painfully.

This program distills that experience into structured, practical learning for engineers, team leads, PMs, and aspiring delivery leaders who want to ship better outcomes — consistently.

---

## 🎯 Target Audience

- **Team Leads & Tech Leads**: Making the transition from individual contributor to people leader
- **Project & Product Managers**: Building stronger delivery disciplines
- **Delivery Managers**: Scaling their impact beyond a single team
- **Senior Engineers**: Developing leadership presence and stakeholder communication skills

---

## 📚 Key Learning Outcomes

1. Build a **delivery confidence culture**: predictability, transparency, and early risk signaling
2. Master **stakeholder management**: translating technical reality into business language
3. Apply **modern estimation techniques** that account for AI-augmented team velocity
4. Design **effective sprint and release rhythms** for complex, multi-team programs
5. Develop the **leadership presence** to influence without authority across the organization
