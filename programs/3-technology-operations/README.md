# Technology Operations

> *Building high-performance technology teams, delivery centers, and operating models that scale.*

## Overview

Technology capability doesn't emerge from hiring alone. It is built through deliberate design of team structure, delivery model, operating rhythm, and governance. Whether establishing an offshore delivery center, scaling an existing engineering team, or modernizing how an IT organization runs — the fundamentals are the same: clarity of accountability, quality operating standards, and a culture of continuous improvement.

This program covers the operational architecture behind high-functioning technology organizations — drawing on real-world experience setting up and running delivery centers across Southeast Asia and beyond.

---

## 🎯 Target Audience

- **CTOs & IT Directors**: Designing or scaling technology operating models
- **Delivery Directors & VP Engineering**: Accountable for team performance and delivery quality
- **Operations Leaders**: Building governance, process standards, and vendor management
- **Founders & Business Leaders**: Setting up a technology function for the first time

---

## 📚 Key Learning Outcomes

1. Design a **technology operating model** that fits the organization's stage and strategy
2. Set up a **delivery center** — onshore, offshore, or hybrid — from structure to culture
3. Build **engineering governance**: standards, quality gates, performance metrics
4. Implement an **AI-augmented delivery model**: how technology teams evolve with AI tooling
5. Create **talent frameworks**: role definitions, career paths, performance assessment
